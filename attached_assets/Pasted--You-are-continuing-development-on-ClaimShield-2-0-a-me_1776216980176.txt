## ITEM 1 — Claim Form: Missing Required Fields (CRITICAL — Claims reject without these)

Add the following fields to the claim wizard. All map to the existing claims table and 837P generator.

**1a. Claim Frequency Code**
- Location: Primary Insurance section of claim wizard
- Field type: Dropdown
- Options: 1 - Original Claim (default), 7 - Replacement of Prior Claim, 8 - Void/Cancel Prior Claim
- Maps to: CLM05-3 in the 837P generator
- Behavior: When user selects 7 or 8, the "Orig Claim #" field (1b) must auto-appear and become required

**1b. Original Claim Number (Orig Claim #)**
- Location: Primary Insurance section, below Frequency Code
- Field type: Text input
- Visibility: Hidden when Frequency = 1. Required when Frequency = 7 or 8
- Maps to: REF segment with qualifier F8 in the 837P generator

**1c. Homebound?**
- Location: Additional Info tab of claim wizard
- Field type: Radio buttons — Yes / No / N/A
- Default: Yes (for home health practice type)
- Maps to: CLM10 homebound indicator in 837P

**1d. Ordering Provider**
- Location: Providers section of claim wizard (alongside existing Rendering/Billing providers)
- Field type: Provider lookup (same pattern as existing provider fields)
- Required for: Home health and VA Community Care claims
- Maps to: NM1 loop with qualifier DQ in 837P

**1e. DX Pointers per charge line**
- Location: Each service line row in the charge/service line editor
- Field type: Multi-select checkboxes — A, B, C, D (mapping to ICD positions 1–4 entered at top of claim)
- Minimum: 1 pointer required per line
- Maps to: SV1-07 diagnosis code pointers in 837P (colon-separated, e.g., "A:B")

**1f. Delay Reason Code**
- Location: Additional Info tab
- Field type: Dropdown, populated from standard X12 delay reason codes:
  - 1 - Proof of Eligibility Unknown
  - 2 - Authorization Delays
  - 3 - Delay in Certifying Provider
  - 4 - Delay in Supplying Billing Forms
  - 5 - Delay in Delivery of Custom-made Appliances
  - 6 - Third Party Processing Delay
  - 7 - Other
  - 8 - No Delay
  - 9 - Original Claim Rejected
  - 10 - Administration Delay in Prior Authorization Process
  - 11 - Other (specify)
- Maps to: PWK segment or CLM delay reason in 837P

After adding all fields: update the pre-submission validation dialog to check that DX pointers are set on all service lines, and that Orig Claim # is populated when Frequency is 7 or 8.

- One card per ERA received, showing: Payer name, Check/EFT number, Payment date, Total payment amount, Status badge (Unposted / Partially Posted / Posted)
- Click a card to open the ERA detail view

- Header: Payer, Check #, Payment date, Total amount
- One section per claim in the ERA:
  - Claim # | Patient Name | DOS | Billed Amount | Allowed Amount | Paid Amount
  - Expandable: one row per service line showing:
    - CPT code | Billed | Allowed | Paid | Adjustment amount | CARC code + description (pulled from existing carc_codes table) | RARC code + description (pulled from existing rarc_codes table)
- Bottom of each claim section: subtotal row

**Per-ERA action buttons:**
- Post This ERA — posts all payments in the ERA to the corresponding claims, updates claim balances, marks ERA as Posted
- Review Manually — marks ERA as "Needs Review", keeps it in the unposted queue
- Skip — removes from current view without posting

**Per-line adjustment handling:**
- CO- prefix adjustments (Contractual Obligations) → auto-write off (no biller action needed)
- PR- prefix adjustments (Patient Responsibility) → flag for review, suggest transferring to patient balance
- OA- prefix adjustments (Other Adjustments) → flag for biller decision
- Show inline: the CARC description so billers understand what happened without looking it up

**Auto-post toggle (per payer, in Payer settings):**
Add a toggle to the existing payer settings: "Auto-post clean ERAs" (default OFF). When ON, ERAs with no errors, no denials, and no $0 lines post automatically without biller review.
