   - Loop 2300 REF*G1*[auth_number]
   - Qualifier is definitely "G1" — confirmed via sample EDI in 
     PGBA 837I CG page 20

   PATIENT IDENTIFIER (Loop 2010CA NM109):
   PGBA accepts three formats per error codes LFN/LFM/SSC/SSE:
   - 9-byte SSN — qualifier "SY" in NM108
   - 10-byte EDIPI — qualifier "MI" in NM108  
   - 17-byte MVI ICN — qualifier "MI" in NM108
   
   System should prefer EDIPI > MVI ICN > SSN. Send only one.
   Use the field captured at patient intake (per the SSN handling
   prompt I sent earlier).

   BILLING PROVIDER:
   - Loop 2010AA NM109: agency NPI (Chajinel: 1184288680)
   - NM108: XX (NPI qualifier)
   - REF*EI: agency tax ID (Chajinel: 47-1075172)

   ENVELOPE:
   - ISA15: P for production, T for test
   - ISA08: 841160004 (PGBA's federal tax ID)
   - GS03: 841160004 (must match ISA08)

   Encode the receiver/payer values as constants in the EDI 
   service since they don't vary by claim — Region 4 is locked 
   in for Chajinel's geography.
